Course Overview

コース概要

🎯 Main Objective主な目的

Understand both the theory and practice of social securities as government spending, with emphasis on efficiency and equity criteria.

理論と実践の両面から社会保障（政府支出）を理解し、効率性と公平性の基準を重視して学ぶ。

⚖️ Two Core Criteria2つの核心基準

Efficiency: Optimal resource allocation through markets and government. Equity: Fair distribution of income and wealth through redistribution.

効率性：市場と政府を通じた資源の最適配分。公平性：所得・富の再分配による公正な分配。

🏛️ 3 Government Functions政府の3機能

① Resource Allocation (efficiency) · ② Income Redistribution (equity) · ③ Macroeconomic Stabilization. Social security primarily relates to function ②.

①資源配分（効率）· ②所得再分配（公平）· ③マクロ経済安定化。社会保障は主に②に関連。
