Q1 Unit 1 Japan's "Lost Growth Period" (1991–2021) average annual real GDP growth was: 日本の「失われた成長期」（1991〜2021年）の実質GDP平均成長率は？

A 5.3%
B 9.1%
C 0.7%
D 2.1%
0.7% — After the asset bubble burst in 1991, Japan stagnated for three decades. 0.7% vs the miracle-era 9.1% illustrates how dramatically growth collapsed.1991年のバブル崩壊後、日本は30年間停滞。高度成長期の9.1%と比較すると成長の落ち込みの大きさがわかる。

Q2 Unit 2 Which of the following is NOT a cause of market failure? 次のうち、市場の失敗の原因でないのはどれか？

A Externalities外部性
B Asymmetric information情報の非対称性
C Perfect competition完全競争
D Market power (monopoly)市場支配力（独占）
Perfect competition完全競争 — Perfect competition IS the benchmark that ensures efficiency. Market failures occur when competition breaks down (monopoly), prices omit social costs (externalities), information is asymmetric, or goods are non-excludable (public goods).完全競争は効率性を保証するベンチマーク。市場の失敗は競争の崩壊（独占）、社会的費用の省略（外部性）、情報の非対称性、非排除性（公共財）のときに生じる。

Q3 Unit 3 Production efficiency (Pareto condition a) requires: 生産効率性（パレート条件a）の要件は？

A MRSA = MRSB
B MRS = MRT
C MRTSXLK = MRTSYLK
D MB = MC
MRTSX = MRTSY — If the MRTS differs between goods X and Y, inputs can be reallocated to produce more of both — a Pareto improvement. Equality eliminates this opportunity.XとYの生産でMRTSが異なれば、投入物の再配分で両財の産出増が可能（パレート改善）。等しくなれば改善余地なし。

Q4 Unit 4 The Rawlsian (Maximin) Social Welfare Function is: ロールズ型（マキシミン）社会厚生関数は？

A W = UA + UB
B W = Min(UA, UB)
C W = Max(UA, UB)
D W = UA × UB
W = Min(UA, UB) — Rawls: maximize the utility of the worst-off person. Social welfare only improves when the least well-off improves. Comes from the "veil of ignorance" — you'd choose this not knowing which person you'd be.ロールズ：最も不遇な人の効用を最大化。最も不遇な人が改善しない限り社会厚生は向上しない。「無知のヴェール」から — 自分がどの立場になるかわからなければこれを選ぶ。

Q5 Unit 5 The Samuelson condition for optimal public goods provision states: 最適公共財供給のサミュエルソン条件は？

A MRSA = MRSB = MRT
B Σ MRSGX = MRTGX
C MBA = MBB = MC
D MRSA × MRSB = MRT
Σ MRS = MRT — Because the same unit of a public good is consumed by ALL individuals simultaneously, its social value is the SUM of individual MRS. This contrasts with private goods where each unit goes to one person (MRS_A = MRS_B = MRT).公共財の同じ単位が全員に同時に消費されるため、社会的価値は個人MRSの合計。私的財（1単位が1人へ）のMRS_A = MRS_B = MRTと対照的。
